# Excel\_Chart.top property

Represents the distance, in points, from the top edge of the object to the top of row 1 (on a worksheet) or the top of the chart area (on a chart). 

 \[Api set: ExcelApi 1.1\]

**Signature:**
```javascript
top: number
```
